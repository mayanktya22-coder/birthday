Happy Birthday Maitri 🫀

I know we haven’t been talking for a very long time, but still, I want you to know that I’m here for you. Whenever I don’t talk to you, nothing really feels right, but the moment we talk, everything suddenly feels beautiful again.

Today is your day, your birthday, so I hope you enjoy every second of it, laugh a lot, and make unforgettable memories. I wish I could do so much more for you today, but this distance between us doesn’t let me. Still, I feel like distance doesn’t really matter when two people truly connect through their vibes, and with you, I feel the most comfortable and peaceful.

You’re an amazing poet, Maitri. Every time I look at the night sky, I remember how much you love the moon and stars. I remember every little thing you tell me. Your words always stay with me because you are truly special to me.

You might wonder why I care so much or why I do all this for you. Honestly, I don’t fully know either. I just know that you mean a lot to me.

Happy Birthday, Bonu Devi, my Devi 🤣🤣

Happy Birthday, Maitri — my tiny little star,

No matter the distance, I’m never too far.

You love the moon, the silence, the skies full of light,

And somehow you make life feel just as bright.


You may be short, but your heart is rare,

The kind that makes people feel safe and cared.

Your yapping, your laughs, your random late-night talks too,

Are honestly some of my favorite things about you.


Tell me your secrets, your fears, your messiest side,

I’ll always be someone standing by your side.

So keep shining softly, just the way you do,

Happy Birthday, Maitri — the stars look prettier with you.
